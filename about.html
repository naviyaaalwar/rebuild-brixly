Our Purpose

Why We Founded Rebuild

The current construction industry is built on toxic materials and destructive production habits. Every year, the manufacturing of traditional materials like concrete and mortar pump massive amounts of greenhouse gases into our atmosphere. Rebuild aims to tackle both of these crises at once. Our goal is to address construction pollution and the rehabilitation crisis in one brick.
