Introducing Brixly

Brixly was inspired by LEGO, featuring an interlocking mechanism that allows our design to attach to itself rather than requiring additional materials.

Our Innovation

The Eco-Brick: Brixly

Brixly aims to help involuntarily displaced residents after natural disasters by providing quick shelter in times of need while using sustainable materials for construction. In contrast to traditional brick designs, our product consists of plastic waste from landfills and natural disaster sites plus natural fibers, helping solve two world problems at once.
